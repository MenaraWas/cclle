Center for Children’s Literature and Literacy Education

Center for Children’s Literature and Literacy Education (CCLLE) of Yogyakarta State University is established in May 2021 in collaboration with Muenster University and DAAD to stimulate the pleasure of reading for the development of literacy among Indonesian children and the world. Literacy skill will be well established if it is stimulated at the early age, as early as possible. That’s why the role of children’s literature is so huge. Children’s literature is the seed of reading and literacy. Unfortunately, in Indonesia, the awareness in the power of children stories such as great picture books and bedtime stories to lay the foundation of literacy is far from what it should be. Children’s literature is suffering from long negligence in quality. There has been a relatively positive development in the writing of Indonesian children’s literature recently, but still plenty, diverse and attractive more are required.

CCLLE is here to educate everyone close to children in the heart, mind and distance to contribute to the development of literacy education through great children’s literature. CCLLE activities include: educating young people on the importance of children’s literature, stimulating them to write good children’s literature dedicated for children, assisting parents and elementary school teachers to help children love reading and therefore gain literacy skills and many more.
